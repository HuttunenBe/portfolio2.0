About me

Hi, I’m Beniitta Huttunen, a Full Stack Web Development student at Business College Helsinki with a strong love for coding.

I’ve always been someone who puts both quality and heart into everything I do. I’m driven by problem-solving, especially when things get challenging, and I always try to give my best no matter the task or situation.

CV

Beniitta Huttunen

What really draws me to web development is how it’s always changing. There’s always something new to learn and new problems to figure out. If coding were easy, I probably wouldn’t enjoy it as much. It’s the challenge that keeps me going.

Before getting into tech, I worked in social services supporting people with mental health challenges. That experience taught me the value of empathy, patience and how small things like positive feedback can make a real difference. I believe kindness and care help build better teams and a better world.

Now I bring that same mindset into web development, combining curiosity, empathy and a drive to not just write code but to do it well.
